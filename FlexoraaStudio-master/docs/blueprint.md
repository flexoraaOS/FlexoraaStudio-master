# **App Name**: Flexoraa AI Automation

## Core Features:

- Homepage Design: Landing page showcasing the AI-powered business automation suite with a hero section, 'About' section, AI suite models (LeadOS and AgentOS), testimonials, and a call-to-action banner.
- LeadOS Page: LeadOS page detailing its features such as lead verification, WhatsApp engagement, lead scoring, and appointment booking, along with pricing plans and CTAs.
- AgentOS Page: AgentOS page describing its multi-channel engagement capabilities, including WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, and Gmail automation, along with features, pricing, and CTAs.
- LeadOS Dashboard: Client dashboard for LeadOS, providing an overview of leads uploaded, verified, and qualified, along with engagement status, campaign summary, and various trackers and reports. Modern styles.
- AgentOS Dashboard: Client dashboard for AgentOS, showing quick stats on new conversations, AI-handled conversations, bookings, and status indicators for connected channels and AI mode. Modern Styles.
- Lead Scoring: WhatsApp AI conversation and lead scoring tool which processes uploaded customer information in CSV format. It utilizes the GPT model to create tags that assign leads based on HOT, WARM, or COLD statuses in the sales pipeline.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: White to convey a clean and modern aesthetic.
- Background color: Black to create a sleek and sophisticated backdrop.
- Accent color: Gradient burning red for interactive elements and highlights, providing a visually striking contrast.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', sans-serif, for a computerized, techy feel, well-suited to headlines and short blocks of text. Body font: 'Inter', sans-serif, for longer blocks of text.
- Use a grid-based layout with ample white space to create a clean, organized interface. Implement a card-based design for presenting information in a structured and digestible manner.
- Employ a consistent set of minimalist icons that reflect the themes of automation, intelligence, and connectivity. Icons should be modern, geometric, and easily recognizable.
- Incorporate subtle animations to enhance the user experience, such as transitions, hover effects, and progress indicators. Use 3D effects sparingly to highlight key visuals.
